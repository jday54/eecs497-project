MEDICAL TERMS
  • defenestration - the act of throwing someone or something out of a window
  • persnickety - characterized by excessive attention to trivial details
  • anomie - lack of moral standards in a society
  • docent - a lecturer or teacher at some universities
  • discombobulate - cause to be confused emotionally
  • bowdlerize - edit by omitting or modifying parts considered indelicate
  • duplicitous - marked by deliberate deceptiveness
  • precis - a sketchy summary of the main points of an argument
  • otiose - serving no useful purpose; having no excuse for being
  • non sequitur - a reply that has no relevance to what preceded it
